Guitar Vocabulary

Fret - A fret is a raised element on the neck of a guitar. Pressing a string against a fret changes the pitch of the note.

Strumming - Strumming is the action of sweeping your hand across the strings to play chords rhythmically.

Acoustic Guitar Strings - The six strings of a standard acoustic guitar, from thickest to thinnest, are:

  • 6th String (Low E) - The lowest-pitched string
  • 5th String (A) - The second-lowest pitch
  • 4th String (D)
  • 3rd String (G)
  • 2nd String (B)
  • 1st String (High e) - The highest-pitched string
